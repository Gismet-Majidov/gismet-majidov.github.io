**Which of the following words does not belong in this group?**

**A) Rare**

**B) Regular**

**C) Exceptional**

**D) Occasional**

<details>
<summary>Reveal Answer</summary>
Answer is B. Other words imply the meaning of not occuring frequently
</details>
